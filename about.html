About the Author

Abhinav Acharya

Hello everyone, welcome to my blog. Today, I'm diving into the fascinating world of trash, waste, garbage, rubbish, and all those things we discard without much thought. But before I toss these items away, I want to take a moment to reflect on their significance in my life and how they've served me. Inspired by experts like Brian Thill and Sarah Newman, who have delved deep into the complexities of waste, I have also embarked on a personal journey to document my trash and explore its meaning.
