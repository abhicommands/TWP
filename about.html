About the Author

Abhinav Acharya

Hello Everyone, I am Abhinav Acharya, I am a junior at Rutgers studying Math and Computer Science. Let me tell you briefly about myself. I am a semi-professional musician and I have been playing tabla for the past 8 years and piano for the past 2 years. I have been playing tabla at various events and concerts around the tristate area. I also am a decent piano player, and I try to make my own music as well. I am also a computer programmer and I participate in competitions and hackathons around Rutgers and the tristate area, I want to try and combine music with computer science and create something new and innovative. I am currently a member of Rutgers Dhol Effect, a band where I play tabla and perform at various events. You can find more about Rutgers Dhol Effect on their Instagram page. If you want we are also going to be performing at the Rutgers Day on April 27, 2024 make sure to check us out!.
